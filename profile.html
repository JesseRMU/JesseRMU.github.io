About Me

My passion for music started when I was eight years old. My older brothers listened to a lot of hardstyle, and one of them was even a hardstyle DJ. He taught me how to produce hardstyle myself when I was eight. Since then, music has become an important part of my life. Nowadays, I work more seriously on music by supporting artists who don’t have the time to make music themselves. I also work with different labels to get the most out of the artists. It gives me great satisfaction to use my creativity and help others realize their musical vision.

Besides music, I had a strong passion for motocross. I started motocross when I was seven and stopped when I was sixteen. Despite my enthusiasm to be active at a high level, I wasn't able to fully realize that ambition. Recently, my participation was hindered by a broken leg, which made it difficult to pick up the sport again. However, I still remain fascinated by the adrenaline and challenge of motocross. Maybe I will take it up again in the future, but the lessons I learned from motocross—perseverance and patience—still help me in my studies and daily life.
