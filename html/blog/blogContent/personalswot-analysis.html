SWOT Analysis

To help me figure out my path in ICT, I did a SWOT analysis. This means looking at my strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Here’s what I found:

Strengths

One of my biggest strengths is that I’m very precise. When I do something, I make sure to do it well. I also think I’m quite creative and good at solving problems. In music production, being able to think outside the box is super helpful, and I believe my creativity helps me a lot.

Weaknesses

On the other side, I know I have some weaknesses. I often have trouble starting tasks on time and planning them out. I tend to begin things late, and I don’t always work as fast as I’d like. I know that improving my planning skills is important, especially in ICT.

Opportunities

Looking ahead, I see many opportunities for myself. I’m not exactly sure what I want to do in the future, but I know it will involve computers. The HBO-ICT program I’m studying is giving me the skills I need for such a career. Topics like programming and networking are preparing me for whatever comes next.

Threats

There are also some threats I need to think about. Sometimes, my motivation isn’t very high, and I struggle a lot with planning. This can make it hard for me to finish my study. Plus, the fast pace of technology and competition in ICT can be challenging. Another significant threat is the rise of artificial intelligence. As AI technology advances, there’s a risk that many traditional ICT jobs could become unnecessary, making it crucial for me to adapt and continuously update my skills.
