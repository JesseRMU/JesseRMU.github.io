My Programming Experience

When I started my HBO-ICT program, I had no programming experience at all. In fact, I didn’t even know how to open my code editor in the beginning. It felt like a completely new world for me.

So far, I’ve learned HTML and CSS, and we are currently working with JavaScript. But honestly, I find it quite difficult. JavaScript still doesn’t come easy to me, and sometimes it can be frustrating. I have learned how to effectively search for information and how to ask others for help when I get stuck.

Sometimes, I struggle with motivation. Still, I push myself to go to school every day and work on my assignments. I know that practice is important to improve my programming skills.

Overall, my programming journey is full of challenges, but I’m determined to keep learning and growing in this field. I look forward to seeing where my skills will take me in the future!
