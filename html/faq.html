FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Go to hz.print.nl, enter your details, and select the file you want to print.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan, you need at least €0.07 on your card. Register your HZ card via the RFID reader on the printer. Then, click on the 'Scan' option and follow the steps as shown there.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Visit https://webshop.hz.nl to browse the webshop.

How can you book a project space?

Log in and go to the Selfservice Portal in your HZ Portal. In the Selfservice Portal, click 'Make a reservation' to book a room of your choice.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is available at the Poelendaelesingel 12 in Middelburg. It's just a few minutes' walk to the HZ building. Unfortunately, parking directly at the HZ building is not possible. There is a disabled parking spot right next to the HZ building at Het Groene Woud 1-3. Students and staff can enter the HZ parking area with an HZ pass, while guests can ring the bell at the barrier.
